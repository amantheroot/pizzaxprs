ABOUT US

We are a group of pizza lovers in Pune, India.

Everybody loves pizza. No doubt in that, we just happen to love it so much that we want to share it with everyone. That's how Pizza Xprs was born, the train which will deliver love from us to you in the form of pizza.

Our pizza is made from hand stretched slow dough which gives it a nice & crispy thin base. Our motto "Only Fresh Ingredients Allowed", and yes, who can forget the cheese? The amount of cheese we put in the pizza is equal to the amount of love we put in it as well :)

Pizza Xprs is located in Hadapsar, Pune. You can find us at Uber Eats and Zomato. Please follow us on Twitter, Instagram and Facebook to be up to date with the latest news.
